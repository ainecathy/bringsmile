Can you bring smile into people's life?

HIV/AIDS and violent conflicts are spawning a massive generation of orphans in Sub-Saharan Africa. The region currently has more than 12 million orphans who have lost either their mother or both parents, most of them under age 15. And the worst is yet to come. Coping with the risks and consequences of orphanhood poses immense problems for Sub-Saharan Africa. Resources are limited, communities are being overwhelmed, and the realization of International Development Goals is being threatened. Yet, the contours of public action are unclear because of the limited knowledge of the scale of the problem and a lack of clarity on the effectiveness of interventions. But together we can make a big difference and bring smile to these children. Act generously, be a super-hero to this group of people by donating; it can be an amount of money or set of good.
